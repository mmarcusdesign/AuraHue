AuraHue Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

AuraHue is primarily a utility website. We do not require registration or collect personally identifiable information (PII) like names, email addresses, or payment details directly from users, except for the use of our services as described below.

2. Data Generated by AI Services

  • **Prompts and Image Data:** When you use our "Generate Palette from Text" or "Generate Palette from Image" features, your input (text prompt and uploaded image) is sent to Google's generative AI models for processing.
  • **Data Storage:** This input data and the resulting color palettes are processed temporarily. We do not store this input data on our servers long-term.
  • **Non-Personal Data:** We collect standard, non-personal data (browser type, time spent on pages, referral sources) via analytics services to improve the user experience.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

AuraHue uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.

  • **DART Cookie:** Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to AuraHue and other sites on the Internet.
  • **Opt-Out:** Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • **Other Third-Party Cookies:** Other third-party advertisers may also use cookies to track user activities. AuraHue has no access to or control over these cookies.

4. Links to External Sites

Our service may contain links to other sites. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or practices of any third-party sites.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
